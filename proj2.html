About The Project

The requirement of the project was to automate the process and generate Navarik(Cargo Inspection Management Software) inspection invoices from Navarik to SAP directly so that the accountant no longer has to manually enter the data. This was achieved using Microsoft’s BizTalk (used to automate business process execution by connecting different systems).

Initial State Of The Project

Previously, inspection vendors (third party companies) were billing supply & trading unit for providing inspection services for the crude / refined products movements via vessels.The billing was in the form of an invoice, which was submitted by inspection companies through the Navarik system (cargo inspection management software).When the billing took place, accounting entries were recorded onto supply & trading unit books by creating third party accounts payable to inspection vendor companies. Earlier, for one business unit, accountants had to make manual entries in SAP-FI module (deals with managing financial accounting) for calculating the inspection costs.

The Process

The invoices from Navarik were fetched by BizTalk and then internal logic was run to capture different fields, which were required by SAP-FI and finally convert the input schema in a format, which the SAP-FI system understands.
